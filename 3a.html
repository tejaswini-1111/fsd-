How to Make a 2-Minute Omelette

Ingredients

  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • A pinch of salt&pepper

Instructions

  1. Whisk the eggs in a small div or bowl.
  2. pour eggs and salt,pepper in a bowl.
  3. whisk them well until 2 mins.
  4. Melt butter in a pan.
  5. Pour eggs in and cook for 2 minutes.

Chef's Tip

Add a little bit of cheese right before folding for extra flavor!

Note: Be careful with the stove!
